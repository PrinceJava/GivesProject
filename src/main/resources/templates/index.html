How it works

Donate in 3 simple steps

01

Search up your favorite charity/cause

With Gives, you only need to search up your favorite charity or cause. Once you have selected feel free to review previous comments and donations, as well as a description of the charity or cause as well as their goal.

02

Use PayPal to donate

Once you have found the charity or because you want to donate too, login to PayPal or checkout as a guest. When the transaction is completed, your account will be authorized for your selected amount until the charity collects those funds.

03

Keep watching your charity until they reach their goal

You can see your charity or causes progress to completing their goal
